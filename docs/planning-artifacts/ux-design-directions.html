📊 Evaluation Criteria

⭐ Revenue Command Feed — CHOSEN DIRECTION

Hybrid ③+⑥. Chronological feed + hero ₦ + command bar. Inline draft expansion with tone chips (Friendly/Pidgin/Formal). Manual reply detection auto-dismisses AI option. Contextual analytics are embedded in feed/home/inbox; detailed analytics pages are post-MVP.

Best for: Fast, minimal, money-first - makes every interaction feel powerful and effortless

① Clean Dashboard

Structured, familiar. Revenue + leads in one view. Risk: too "app-like", not conversational enough.

Best for: Users who want overview first, act second

② Conversation-First

Opens in the DM Close flow. PrimingCard + AI draft = core experience front and center. Most aligned with defining experience.

Best for: Maximizing the DM Close — the money moment

③ Revenue-Focused

BIG money number. Every action tied to ₦ impact. Motivating. Risk: analytics-first feeling.

Best for: "I feel powerful" emotional goal — money visibility

④ WhatsApp-Native

Zero learning curve. Feels like a WhatsApp upgrade. AI is invisible infrastructure. Risk: looks like a clone.

Best for: Adoption speed — sellers already know how to use it

⑤ Card-Based Actions

Swipeable, engaging, TikTok-energy. Each card = one action NOW. Risk: too many cards = overwhelm.

Best for: Engagement — makes business feel like social media

⑥ Minimalist Command

Clean stream, command bar for power users. Everything in one feed. Risk: not visual enough for fashion sellers.

Best for: Power users — efficiency over decoration
